Alex is a young and successful Indonesian businessman who takes pride in his successes and lavinsh life to the extent that it blinds him from the people whom he owed his successes to.
Knowing the situation, a kind-hearted mentalist helps him by making him live in his own dream in Cirebon, Indonesia in the 1800s - the setting of the longest forgotten history in Indonesia.
Although Alex is wowed by the uniqueness of Indonesias arts and culture,he faces many challenges which put his love for the one and only to the test.
